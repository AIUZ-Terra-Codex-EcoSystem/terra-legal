Terra Legal

Compact legal, governance, publication, and protocol donor layer for the wider Terra ecosystem. This page is a readable public entrance into the explicit normative spine, not the whole archive.

What it is

A compact perimeter for rules, participation, governance, publication, and the explicit Terra legal-operational spine.

What it is not

Not the entire Terra archive, not a dump zone, and not a replacement for the full FMP corpus.

Why it matters

It gives other live repositories one coherent donor layer instead of leaving legal and protocol logic scattered across mirrors and drafts.

Use in the Ecosystem

  • Live repositories may inherit files directly from this donor layer.
  • They may adapt them with local bridge language when needed.
  • Compact normative files belong here; heavy engines and raw mirrors do not.
  • The wider hidden standards layer continues to be reviewed and normalized around this core.

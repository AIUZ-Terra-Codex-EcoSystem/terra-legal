# PUBLICATION RULE

Publication boundary for the Terra legal donor repository.

## What belongs here

- stable legal texts;
- governance documents;
- participation rules;
- security and reporting guidance;
- bridge files that other repositories can inherit.

## What does not belong here

- raw archive dumps;
- duplicate theory corpora;
- temporary exports;
- auto-generated noise;
- private operational secrets.

## Publication principle

Public material should be:

1. intentional;
2. readable;
3. reviewable by humans;
4. safe for open publication;
5. linked to a clear repository role.

## Cross-repository rule

If a file is primarily theoretical, move or mirror it into a theory repository.
If it is primarily a corpus or evidence object, keep it in a corpus repository.
If it is only shadow or backup material, keep it outside the public legal layer.
